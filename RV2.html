PERCEPTION MANAGEMENT

Perception is how we think about a particular person, situation, event, or anything for that matter based on the stimuli we receive and the feelings and thoughts that we have about that particular thing. Perception means what we want to see and sometimes what we want to see has nothing to do with the truth.Life is alll about perceptions,positive vs negative. Whichever we chose will affect us and more than that reflect our outcomes.
